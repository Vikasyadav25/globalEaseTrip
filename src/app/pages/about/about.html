Our Story

Founded in 2024, GlobalEaseTrip was born from a simple yet powerful vision: to create extraordinary travel experiences that anyone can enjoy. What began as a passion project by a small team of travel enthusiasts has quickly grown into a trusted name in personalized travel planning.

We believe that travel can transform lives, open minds, and create memories that last a lifetime. Our mission is to curate authentic, immersive journeys that go far beyond typical tourist routes.

In just our first year, we’ve proudly served 50+ happy clients, crafting unique itineraries across some of the world’s most breathtaking destinations. And this is just the beginning — every trip we design is infused with care, creativity, and a deep love for exploration.
